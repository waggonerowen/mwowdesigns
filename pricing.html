Common questions

Who owns the website?

On the one-time plan, everything is yours the day we launch — the site, the design, the domain. On the monthly plan, we host and maintain the site while you're subscribed, and your content and domain always belong to you. Want to own it outright later? You can buy it out anytime.

What if I want to cancel the monthly plan?

After the first 12 months you can cancel anytime — no penalties, no hard feelings. We'll help you take your content and domain with you, or you can buy out the site and keep it running yourself.

What counts as a "small edit"?

Text changes, new photos, updated hours, prices, menus, staff, seasonal specials — the everyday stuff. Bigger jobs like brand-new pages or a redesign we'll quote you first, so nothing is ever a surprise.

How fast do edits happen?

Usually the same or next business day. You text or email us, we make the change, done — that's the whole point of the monthly plan.

Can I switch plans later?

Yes. Start monthly and buy it out whenever you like, or start with the one-time build and add a monthly care plan down the road. We'll always give you a clear number first.
